Changing the CR will change all other values to the CR average. Changing the other sliders will update the "Points to Spend" to match the current CR. Changing sliders more than a couple points will severly imbalance the challenge rating.

A balanced but challenging encounter should be approximately half the sum of the party's levels in CR. Example: 4 level 10 PCs should be able to fight 1 level 20 CR creature or 2 level 10 CR creatures.

This calculates 1 point per:
  • 7.5 hp
  • 3 damage
  • 1 ac
  • (1 to hit + 1 dc) / 2
